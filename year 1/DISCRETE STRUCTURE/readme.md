## Course Overview

This course introduces mathematical structures used in computer science, such as logic, sets, relations, functions, graphs, trees, counting methods, and proof techniques.

---

## Reflection

Discrete Structure helped me understand the mathematical foundation behind computer science. At first, some topics felt abstract, but over time I realised that concepts such as logic, sets, relations, functions, and graphs are closely related to programming and problem solving.

The course improved my ability to think clearly and justify solutions. Learning proof techniques, counting principles, graph theory, and trees helped me develop a more systematic way of analysing problems instead of depending only on memorisation.

Overall, this course strengthened my logical thinking and mathematical reasoning. It provides an important foundation for algorithms, data structures, database theory, networking, and many other computing subjects.

---

## Key Takeaways

- Improved understanding of logic, sets, relations, and functions.
- Learned graph and tree concepts used in computing.
- Developed stronger reasoning and proof skills.
- Built foundation for algorithm and data structure learning.

---

## Conclusion

In conclusion, **Discrete Structure** has provided useful knowledge and skills that are important for my academic development and future career. The course helped me improve my understanding, strengthen my learning foundation, and become more prepared to apply these concepts in real-world computing and professional situations.
