## Course Overview

This course exposes students to entrepreneurship concepts in technology, including innovation, business thinking, product ideas, and the role of technology in creating value.

---

## Reflection

Technopreneurship Seminar gave me useful exposure to the relationship between technology and entrepreneurship. It helped me understand that technical knowledge can also be transformed into products, services, or solutions that create value for society and business.

The seminar encouraged me to think beyond academic learning and consider how innovation, problem-solving, and creativity can be applied in the business world. It also increased my awareness of the mindset needed by entrepreneurs, such as initiative, adaptability, communication, and willingness to take calculated risks.

Overall, this course broadened my perspective on career possibilities in technology. It inspired me to think more creatively about using technical skills not only for employment, but also for innovation and future opportunities.

---

## Key Takeaways

- Understood the connection between technology and entrepreneurship.
- Learned the importance of innovation and business thinking.
- Improved awareness of entrepreneurial mindset and opportunity creation.
- Broadened perspective on future career and venture possibilities.

---

## Conclusion

In conclusion, **Technopreneurship Seminar** has provided useful knowledge and skills that are important for my academic development and future career. The course helped me improve my understanding, strengthen my learning foundation, and become more prepared to apply these concepts in real-world computing and professional situations.
