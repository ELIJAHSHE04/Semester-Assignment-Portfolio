## Course Overview

This course introduces the basic principles of quantity surveying, including construction measurement, cost estimation, project documentation, and the role of quantity surveyors in construction projects.

---

## Reflection

Introduction to Quantity Surveying helped me understand the role of cost planning and measurement in construction projects. Although this course is different from my main computing field, it gave me useful exposure to how project cost, materials, and documentation are managed in another professional area.

Through this course, I learned that quantity surveying requires accuracy, responsibility, and good understanding of project requirements. The course also showed me how estimation and measurement are important for controlling cost and supporting project decisions.

Overall, this course broadened my perspective by exposing me to knowledge outside computer science. It helped me appreciate the importance of project planning, cost awareness, and professional responsibility in real-world industries.

---

## Key Takeaways

- Understood the basic role of quantity surveying.
- Learned the importance of cost estimation and measurement.
- Improved awareness of project planning and documentation.
- Gained exposure to construction-related professional knowledge.

## Conclusion

In conclusion, **Introduction to Quantity Surveying** has provided useful knowledge and skills that are important for my academic development and future career. The course helped me improve my understanding, strengthen my learning foundation, and become more prepared to apply these concepts in real-world and professional situations.
